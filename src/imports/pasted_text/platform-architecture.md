Section 4 — Flywheel Concept

Headline:
Why the Platform Gets Stronger Over Time

Body copy:
The platform is designed around flywheels — self-reinforcing loops where better data improves models, better models improve production efficiency, and stronger governance expands what data can be used safely and effectively.

Purpose:
Explain the flywheel concept in a way that feels intuitive and understandable.

Use 3 flywheel concepts:

Data Flywheel
AI pre-annotation and human refinement improve production efficiency, while the resulting data continuously improves the annotation models. The more high-quality data accumulates, the better the system performs.

Synthetic Flywheel
Synthetic data helps cover rare, dangerous, or expensive-to-capture scenarios. Real-world comparison and human-labeled differences then improve simulation quality over time.

Compliance Flywheel
Trusted collaboration environments, federated learning, and expert ecosystems make more data usable across more participants — which in turn increases the platform’s value and expands what can be built.

Each flywheel should feel like a real mechanism, not just a label.
Use circular flow arrows, looping paths, or reinforcing motion cues.

Important:
This is one of the key educational sections of the page.
Make it intuitive, strategic, and visually memorable.

Micro interactions:
	•	subtle loop animation or hover emphasis on flywheel arrows
